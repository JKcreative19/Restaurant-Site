Once upon a time, in this place, there was a cozy cellar where friends would end up at the end of the day to tell each other ancient and new tales, to exchange mottoes and jokes; here also the loves were born.

Taken from the old name of the street where the restaurant is located, in fact until the beginning of the 1500s, the current Via Farrattini, was Via Amelia and, in the Amerine habits, remained with this name.

The spirit with which we work is to create a warm and simple atmosphere at the same time, in short, to make our customers feel at ease. Cordiality, kindness, positive energy, together with dishes with genuine and particular flavors are our peculiarities.
